Interaction Rules

Do

  • Use token variables before adding new colors.
  • Keep feed scan density high and card chrome low.
  • Use explicit click intent in extension interactions.
  • Preserve keyboard and focus accessibility paths.

Do Not

  • Hijack normal link clicks with panel behavior.
  • Mix bright gradients into paper mode layouts.
  • Hide critical controls behind hover-only behavior.
  • Introduce one-off accent colors per component.

Motion

Use subtle linear motion for ambient status surfaces only.
